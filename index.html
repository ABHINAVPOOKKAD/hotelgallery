ABOUT US

Salkara's irresistible appeal comes from the flavour of nostalgia that pervades every dimension of this unique concept restaurant. Based on classic recipes popular during the glorious 70s and 80s in Malabar, the restaurant's cuisine features wholesomely delicious treats true to the happy memories of time gone by.
